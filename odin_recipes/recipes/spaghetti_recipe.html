Mom's Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Parmesan
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. Fry onion until translucent
  2. Add garlic to pan
  3. Add tomato sauce to mixture
  4. Add italian seasoning to sauce
  5. Pour sauce over spaghetti
  6. Top with parmesan and basil
